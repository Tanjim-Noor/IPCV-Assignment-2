### Describing Trends and Projections

A trend is the general direction in which something is developing or changing over time. A projection
is a prediction of future change. Trends and projections are usually illustrated using line graphs in
which the horizontal axis represents time. Some of the language commonly used for writing about
trends and projections is given below.
