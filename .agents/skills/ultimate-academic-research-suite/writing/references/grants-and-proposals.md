# Grants and Research Proposals

## Contents

- Opportunity fit
- Aims
- Narrative
- Approach
- Impact and implementation
- Budget
- Compliance

## Opportunity fit

Verify the current call, eligibility, scope, evaluation criteria, budget ceiling, duration, required forms, and deadline from the official funder source. Build a compliance matrix before drafting.

## Specific aims

State:

1. The important problem.
2. The gap or unmet need.
3. The long-term goal and current objective.
4. Central hypothesis or guiding premise where appropriate.
5. Two to four coherent aims.
6. Expected outcomes and impact.

Each aim should be valuable but not make all later aims fail automatically if it is unsuccessful.

## Narrative

### Significance

Explain the problem, affected stakeholders, current limitations, and why solving it matters. Support scale and burden claims.

### Innovation

Identify what is conceptually, methodologically, technically, or practically new. Do not equate novelty with value.

### Approach

For each aim provide rationale, design, sample/data, measures, analysis, milestones, risks, alternatives, ethics, and expected outcomes. Distinguish preliminary evidence from proposed work.

### Team and environment

Connect expertise, roles, facilities, partnerships, governance, and access to the proposed work. Avoid generic capability claims.

## Impact and implementation

Define outputs, outcomes, beneficiaries, uptake pathway, evaluation measures, sustainability, and limitations. For broader-impact requirements, address the exact funder criterion rather than adding generic outreach.

## Budget

Align every cost with an activity and timeline. Separate personnel, equipment, supplies, travel, participant costs, services, data/software, dissemination, and indirect costs as the scheme requires. Never invent rates or institutional policies.

## Compliance

Check page limits, fonts, attachments, biosketches, letters, data management, open access, ethics, security, conflicts, and AI-use rules. Mark institution-dependent sections for authorized administrative review.
